Empowering Widows. Caring for Orphans.

The Racheal Emmanuel Foundation provides essential health outreach, skills training for widowed mothers, and welfare support for orphans in local communities.

Learn Our Mission

Our Core Focus

Three Pillars of Impact

Widow Empowerment

Providing vocational training, business grants, and mentorship to help widowed women achieve financial independence and stability for their families.

Orphan Welfare

Ensuring shelter, education, clothing, and nutritional support for orphaned children, giving them a foundation for a successful future.

Community Health

Organizing regular free medical check-ups, blood pressure screenings, and deworming programs to promote preventative health in rural areas.

Lives Transformed

Our Latest Success Stories

These are the faces and families whose futures you helped redefine through your generous support.

Mercy's New Bakery

#WidowEmpowerment

Left without income, **Mercy** received intensive baking training and a micro-grant from REF. She now runs a small but thriving bakery stall, providing for her three children and even hiring an assistant. Her dignity and financial independence have been fully restored.

Daniel's Scholarship

#OrphanWelfare

After losing both parents, **Daniel** was struggling to stay in school. REF stepped in to cover his full tuition, uniforms, and books. He is now a top student in his class, dreaming of becoming an engineer. Education is his path to breaking the cycle.

Mama Tunde's Vital Check

#CommunityHealth

**Mama Tunde**, 78, had never had a medical check-up. During an REF outreach event, her dangerously high blood pressure was identified. She received free medication and lifestyle advice, dramatically improving her quality of life and longevity.
